# Website Design Specification: CAM2COOK Landing Page

**Project Name:** CAM2COOK
**Tagline:** Your Real-Time AI Sous-Chef. From Fridge to Feast.
**Target Vibe:** Modern, sleek, interactive, and appetizing. A mix of high-tech AI (AR overlays, scanning UI) and warm culinary visuals.
**Color Palette:** - Primary: #FF6B6B (Vibrant Coral/Tomato Red - appetizing and energetic)
- Secondary: #4ECDC4 (Fresh Mint/Teal - clean, tech-forward)
- Background: #0F172A (Deep Slate/Dark Mode - makes the food and AI overlays pop)
- Text: #F8FAFC (Off-white for readability)

---

## 1. Global Elements
- **Typography:** - Headers: 'Outfit' or 'Inter' (Bold, modern sans-serif)
  - Body: 'Roboto' or 'Open Sans' (Clean, readable)
- **Navigation Bar (Sticky, Glassmorphism effect):**
  - Left: Logo text `[ CAM2COOK ]` with a minimalist camera lens/plate icon.
  - Center Links: `Features`, `How It Works`, `Interactive Demo`, `FAQ`
  - Right: `[ Join Early Access ]` (Button, Primary Color, rounded corners)

---

## 2. Hero Section (The Core Animation)
**Layout:** Split 50/50 on desktop (Left: Text, Right: Interactive Animation). Stacked on mobile.
- **Left Column (Copy):**
  - **Headline (H1):** "Don't Know What to Cook? Let Your Camera Decide."
  - **Sub-headline:** "Point your camera at your fridge, choose a recipe, and let our real-time AI guide your hands through every step of the cooking process."
  - **CTA Buttons:** `[ Try the Web Demo ]` (Primary) `[ Watch Video ]` (Secondary, outlined)
- **Right Column (Hero Animation - CRITICAL FOR AI BUILDER):**
  - **Concept - 3-Part Looping 3D/Isometric Animation:**
    1. **Scene 1 (Scan):** An animated smartphone hovers in front of an open, glowing refrigerator. Bounding boxes (AR UI elements) pop up over tomatoes, chicken, and spinach with scanning sounds/visuals.
    2. **Scene 2 (Decide):** The phone screen morphs into a sleek UI floating in mid-air, displaying 3 recipe cards. A cursor clicks "Tuscan Chicken".
    3. **Scene 3 (Guide):** The camera angle shifts to a kitchen counter. The phone rests on a tripod stand. The camera lens acts as a spotlight, tracking a pair of hands chopping. Floating AR text appears over the cutting board: *"Chop into 1-inch cubes. Perfect!"* with a green checkmark.
  - *Instruction for AI:* Use WebGL or Lottie for this animation. Make the transitions seamless and morphing.

---

## 3. Features Section (Grid Layout)
**Layout:** 3-column grid card layout with hover effects (slight lift, glowing border).
**Section Header (H2):** "Your Kitchen's Smartest Upgrade"
- **Card 1: Smart Fridge Vision**
  - *Icon:* Eye inside a fridge or scanner lines.
  - *Text:* Instantly identifies ingredients, quantities, and freshness just by looking. No manual entry required.
- **Card 2: Contextual Recipe Generation**
  - *Icon:* Magic wand with a chef's hat.
  - *Text:* Generates gourmet, healthy, or quick recipes based strictly on what you *already* have. No grocery runs needed.
- **Card 3: Live AR Cooking Guide**
  - *Icon:* Video camera with a target reticle.
  - *Text:* Places your phone on the counter. The AI watches your progress, tells you when to flip the steak, and corrects your chopping technique in real-time.

---

## 4. "How It Works" Section (Vertical Stepper)
**Layout:** A vertical timeline line down the center. Alternating left/right text and image blocks.
- **Step 1: The Scan.** Image of UI highlighting food with confidence scores (e.g., "Bell Pepper: 98%").
- **Step 2: The Choice.** Image of a Tinder-style swipe interface for recipes. 
- **Step 3: The Setup.** Image illustrating where to place the phone (propped up, viewing the cutting board and stove).
- **Step 4: The Cook.** A GIF/short video showing the AI correcting a user: *"Heat is too high! Lower to medium."*

---

**Layout:** Full-width dark section. 
- **Visual:** A large, realistic tablet mockup in the center.
- **Interaction:** A button saying `[ Simulate Camera Feed ]`. When clicked, the tablet shows a pre-recorded POV video of a kitchen counter. The UI overlays AR graphics (e.g., drawing a circle around a pan, showing a timer counting down directly above the pan).
- **Text Overlay:** "Experience the real-time AI computer vision right in your browser."
