Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a specific phone?

CAM2COOK works on any smartphone with a decent camera and internet connection. iOS 13+ and Android 10+ recommended.

How accurate is the ingredient detection?

Our AI achieves 98%+ accuracy on common ingredients. You can manually add or adjust items if needed.

Does it work with frozen or pre-packaged foods?

Yes! CAM2COOK recognizes all food types, including frozen items, packaged goods, and fresh produce.

Is my cooking process private?

Absolutely. The video feed is processed locally on your device. We never store or share your cooking sessions.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes, you can save, rate, and organize recipes. We'll learn your preferences over time.

What cuisines does CAM2COOK support?

Currently supporting 50+ cuisines with daily additions. Dietary restrictions and allergies are fully supported.
